# Set Dept AI Levels and Roadmap for Automations

## Measurable Outcome
Every department has a defined AI maturity level, a documented roadmap of automation opportunities with sequencing for Q3, and estimated time + dollar savings for each initiative. This becomes the investment roadmap for H2 2026.

## Why This Is a Company Rock
Upfront AI investments now prevent adding headcount later. Each department has untapped automation potential, but without a structured assessment and plan, efforts are ad-hoc. This Rock creates the blueprint for operational leverage across the company.

## Dependencies
- Each department team documenting their current manual steps and time investment
- Department heads participating in assessment and roadmap review
