# Execution Plan: Set Dept AI Levels and Roadmap for Automations

**Rock:** rock-004 — Set Dept AI Levels and Roadmap for Automations
**Owner:** Deuce | **Hard Deadline:** Fri May 29, 2026 | **Weeks:** 9 (Mar 30 → May 29)
**Original due in Rock file:** Jun 30 | **Pulled in to:** May 29 (paternity Jun 1)

## Purpose

Every Recess department has a defined AI maturity level (1–5), a documented roadmap of automation opportunities with Q3 sequencing, and estimated time + dollar savings for each initiative. This becomes the investment roadmap for H2 2026.

## Approach

A 3-skill pipeline (`ai-readiness-assess` → `ai-readiness-score` → `ai-readiness-roadmap`) installed via Recess-Brain-V2 does the work end-to-end. Each team member self-serves their own conversational interview (~25 min); Deuce runs the synthesis and roadmap generation in batched work blocks.

**Source framework:** Greenberg & White, *Digital Operations Playbook* — 5 Levels (Information Silos → Connectable Cloud → Unified Data → Automated Workflows → AI-Powered) + 5 Pillars (Talent / Workflow / Digital Architecture / Knowledge Mgmt / AI Automation) + 30-question self-assessment.

**Two leadership touchpoints:**
1. **In-person AM-only review** — Tue Apr 22 — drives the AM hire decision and unblocks AM execution immediately
2. **Full company leadership review** — Mon-Tue May 18-19 — locks Q3 sequencing across all 8 departments

**Working principle:** Batch Deuce's work into 2-3 hour focused blocks, not 30-min daily sessions. Self-serve removes Deuce from team interviews entirely.
