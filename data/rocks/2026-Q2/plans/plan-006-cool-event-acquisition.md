| Q1 2027 events | Q3-Q4 2026 | Research now, long runway |

### Per-Event Acquisition Workflow (Asana Subtask Template)

Each target event in Asana follows this subtask sequence:

```
☐ Event research + universe mapping (main event, satellites, adjacent partners, alternate paths)
☐ Contact intel + find partnership decision maker
☐ Outreach sequence launched (email, LinkedIn, referrals)
☐ Follow-up / alternate path if no response (referrals from non-target contacts, mutual connections)
☐ Terms negotiation + model fit confirmation (do they have staff to distribute?)
☐ Onboard on platform
☐ Marketing handoff (HubSpot deal card triggers Slack → Asana → Marketing)
```
