Section 1

The Three System Pieces

The entire Recess OS automation layer is just three things: one Python module, one YAML config, and one daily cron. Everything the team touches sits on top as user-facing skills.

The Whole System on a Napkin

recess_os.py     ← One module, every system action
recess_os.yml    ← One config file, every dept/meeting/goal
recess_os_daily  ← One Cloud Scheduler entry, day-of-week dispatch

ceos-meeting-prep ← Interactive prep skill
/meeting-wrap     ← End-of-meeting closed loop
/preread-fill     ← Dept lead helper

Three artifacts power the automation. Three skills power the human surface. That's the whole rock.

The /meeting-wrap Closed Loop

Bi-weekly Thursday meeting. Wraps to next leadership card, posts to #leadership-team.

Section 7

Recess Brain vs BigQuery Split

Two stores, two purposes. Recess Brain is the static catalog — what things are. BigQuery is the dynamic ledger — what happened. Don't mix them.

Recess Brain — Static Catalog

github.com/leo-recess/Recess-Brain-V2

  • KPI definitions
  • Goal metadata
  • Project metadata
  • Skills + agents registry
  • Decisions log

BigQuery — Dynamic Ledger

App_Recess_OS

  • Status updates (timestamped)
  • L10 meetings + transcripts
  • Action items + completion
  • Scorecard time series
  • Every event with a timestamp

Rule of thumb: If it has a timestamp, it goes in BigQuery. If it's a definition or registration, it goes in Recess Brain. When in doubt, ask: "Will this be different next week?" Yes → BQ. No → Brain.

6-Phase Implementation Plan

Eight weeks. Six phases. Three weeks of buffer before paternity leave on June 10. Dept meetings come BEFORE leadership because they have higher leverage and they're the variant the leadership skill builds on.

The Roadmap

Phase 0
Apr 6 — Apr 10

Setup + Foundation

HTML training page (this doc), Asana portfolio, recess_os.yml scaffolded, repo + Cloud project + scheduler entry created. Get the runway clear.

Phase 1
Apr 10 (focus day)

Data Layer + sync_to_bq

BigQuery schemas. App_Recess_OS dataset live. Asana → BQ daily sync running. Recess Brain repo connected. Foundation locked.

Phase 2
Apr 13 — Apr 22

Department Meeting Automation FIRST

Build ceos-l10 parameterized for all 7 depts. /meeting-wrap for dept-l10 type. Higher leverage than leadership — 7 meetings per week vs 1 every other.

Phase 3
Apr 22 — May 1

Leadership Meeting Automation

Variant of dept skills with bi-weekly cadence + Wednesday auto-fill. Founder's Agenda refresh. Pre-read posting. Wednesday memo cron.

Phase 4
May 1 — May 11

Goal Sync + KPI Push

Friday cron pushes KPI snapshot to Asana goals. Project-backed goals get activity synthesis. Threshold rules + status colors live.

Phase 5
May 11 — May 18

Front Door Auto-Routing

Asana intake form → auto-route by request type. Ties Recess OS to the existing Scoping Pipeline architecture.

Phase 6
May 18 — Jun 1

Hardening + Handoff

End-to-end runs, dry runs with each dept, fix the rough edges, document the runbook. 3 weeks of buffer before paternity leave on June 10.

Why dept meetings before leadership? The leadership skill is a variant of the dept skill. Building dept first means leadership is mostly config + a Wednesday cron when we get there. It also means 7 weekly meetings get value before the bi-weekly one does.
Section 9

How To Use It (For The Team)

A practical reference. If you're a Recess team member, this is the part you actually need. Bookmark it.

Quick Reference

If you want to… Run this
Start an L10 for your department ceos-l10 --dept=<your-dept>
Wrap an L10 (or any meeting) /meeting-wrap --type=<dept-l10|leadership|founders|1on1>
Fill in your pre-read section /preread-fill
Find anything in the system Search Recess Brain
See where your action items live Asana subtasks under your meeting card

Before your L10

Run ceos-l10. It pulls your scorecard from BQ, your Rocks from Asana, and assembles the agenda automatically.

After your L10

Run /meeting-wrap. It captures action items as subtasks, creates next week's card, and posts a recap to your channel.

Pre-read deadline

Wednesday 9am for leadership weeks. The cron will ping you if your section is empty. Run /preread-fill to auto-draft.
